The deliciously smelling, spring blooms of the Skeena Cherry are followed by juicy, commercial grade cherries that are ready to harvest during the late summer months. These cherries are large and resistant to cracking, so you get to keep more of the cherries you grow. Plus, they’re ideal for preserving in jams or baking into your favorite cherry recipes. Also, the Skeena Cherry Tree is low maintenance and self-fertile. That means you don’t have to plant this cherry tree with a companion in order to have prolific quantities of fruit. It also means that it's ideal for planting with other cherry trees that aren't self-fertile.
